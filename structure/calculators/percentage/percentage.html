A percentage calculator is a tool that allows you to perform mathematical calculations involving percentages. This type of calculator can be used for a wide range of applications, from basic arithmetic to more advanced calculations such as figuring out discounts, interest rates, and more.

One of the most common uses for a percentage calculator is to determine the percentage of a number. For example, if you want to know what 25% of 100 is, you would input 25 and 100 into the calculator, and the calculator would give you the answer of 25. This type of calculation is useful for determining discounts, tax rates, and other scenarios where you need to know what a certain percentage of a number is.

Another common use for a percentage calculator is to find the percentage increase or decrease between two numbers. For example, if the price of a product increases from $50 to $60, you can use a percentage calculator to determine the percentage increase of the product.

A percentage calculator can also be used to figure out the original number when you only know the percentage and the final number. This can be helpful when trying to figure out the original price of a discounted item or the original interest rate before it was increased.
