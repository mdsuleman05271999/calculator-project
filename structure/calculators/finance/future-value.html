Future Value Calculator




ADD

The future value calculator is a tool that helps to calculate the future worth of an investment or savings account given an initial deposit, interest rate, and the number of years or months it will grow. The formula used to calculate the future value is:

Formula: FV = PV * (1 + r)^t

Where:

FV = future value

PV = present value or initial deposit

r = interest rate as a decimal

t = time in years or months that the investment or savings account will grow

This formula takes into account the principle amount, interest rate, and the length of time the investment is held to determine the future value of the investment at the end of the specified time period. It can be used to estimate the growth of a savings account, investment portfolio, or any other type of investment that earns interest. The future value calculator is a useful tool for financial planning and can help individuals make informed decisions about their investments and savings goals.
