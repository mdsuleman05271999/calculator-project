Simple Interest Calculator

ADD

A simple interest calculator is a tool used to determine the interest earned on an investment over a period of time, without taking into account the effect of compound interest. Simple interest is calculated based solely on the original principal and the interest rate, without considering the accumulation of interest over time.

The formula for calculating simple interest is:

I = (P * r * t)/100

Where:

I = the interest earned

P = the principal (the original amount invested)

r = the annual interest rate

t = the number of years the investment is held

Using a simple interest calculator, you can input the principal, annual interest rate, and the number of years the investment is held, to determine the amount of interest earned.

Simple interest calculators are commonly used for short-term investments, such as savings accounts, where the interest earned is paid at the end of the investment period. They provide a quick and easy way to determine the potential return on a simple interest investment and can be used to compare different investment options.
